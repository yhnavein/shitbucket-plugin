# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

## Overview

Shitbucket is a simple browser extension that customizes the visual appearance of Bitbucket by changing its favicon and logo. This extension operates entirely locally in your browser.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any data whatsoever.**

This extension:
- Does NOT collect any personal information
- Does NOT track your browsing activity
- Does NOT store any user data
- Does NOT communicate with external servers
- Does NOT use cookies or analytics
- Does NOT display advertisements

## What the Extension Does

The extension only performs the following actions locally in your browser:
- Replaces the Bitbucket favicon with a custom icon
- Modifies the Bitbucket homepage logo and text
- All changes are visual only and occur locally on your device

## Permissions

The extension requires permission to access Bitbucket pages solely to apply visual modifications. No data from these pages is collected, stored, or transmitted.

## Third Parties

This extension does not share any information with third parties because it does not collect any information in the first place.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at the GitHub repository with an updated revision date.
